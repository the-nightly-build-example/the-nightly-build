Communication-Deferred Training at Datacenter Scale

L. Osei, M. Tran, P. Varga · arXiv:2506.01847 · 2026

Read the paper
We show that deferring gradient synchronization to off-peak power windows reduces facility draw 47% on a 4,096-GPU cluster while costing 11% throughput, and characterize when the trade favors the deferral.1
