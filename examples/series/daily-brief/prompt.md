# Daily Brief

Tonight's developments, briefed for tomorrow morning. This is NOT a
single-topic digest: cover whichever lanes actually moved today.

Lanes (label each item with its lane; skip any lane with nothing worth
saying):

- research papers, benchmarks, capability results
- compute chips, datacenters, energy, supply chain
- industry products, launches, money, labs
- policy regulation, standards, court rulings
- wildcard anything genuinely important beyond the usual lanes

Emphases:

- Four to eight items, each with a real "why it matters". No filler to reach
  the count; a short honest brief beats a padded one.
- Link each item's headline directly to its primary source, and cite the
  primary announcement or paper over any coverage of it.
- Reference earlier briefs when a story develops ("we have been tracking...").
