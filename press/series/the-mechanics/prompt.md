# The Mechanics

How AI systems actually work, one working part per article, hand-worked, no
code. Anything with a how between the arithmetic and the product is on the
beat. Every part earns its keep with an example small enough to check on
paper. Build on parts already explained, link them, and teach only the new
one.

Architecture, objective, data, decoding, and the product around them are
different things, and the piece keeps them apart. Say what a component
computes, what shaped it, and what could change it. Worked numbers three or
more rows deep go in the worked table; fewer stay in prose.
