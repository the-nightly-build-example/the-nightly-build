# The Rules of AI — series prompt

Decks that argue one thesis about AI governance using ONLY primary regulatory
sources — the frameworks, acts, and orders themselves, not commentary about
them. That constraint is the point: what do the rules actually say?

Emphases:

- Big-stat slides want numbers straight from the texts: article counts,
  compliance deadlines, penalty ceilings, effective dates.
- Quote obligations precisely; never paraphrase a legal requirement loosely.
- One divider slide splitting the argument into its two strongest halves.
- The closing slides state what is binding today vs. voluntary, for whom.
