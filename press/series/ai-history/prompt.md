# A History of Artificial Intelligence — series prompt

Tell the history of AI as a story of ideas colliding with hardware and money.
Each chronicle covers its era along the timeline spine: the bets people made,
why the winters came, what actually changed when they ended.

Emphases:

- Name the people and the papers; date the turning points precisely.
- Treat funding cycles (DARPA, corporate labs, venture) as first-class plot.
- The "Echoes today" section should connect the era's pattern to a live
  question in current AI — not a vague moral, a specific parallel.
