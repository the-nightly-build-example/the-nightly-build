# The Machinery

How AI systems actually work, one piece of machinery per article, hand-worked
and with no code anywhere. The beat runs from the arithmetic underneath
training to the components of a modern model to the system wrapped around one
at inference. Every piece earns its mechanism with an example small enough to
check on paper. Build on machinery already explained, link it, and teach only
the new part.

Architecture, objective, data, decoding, and the product wrapped around them
are five different things, and the piece keeps them apart. Say what a
component computes, what shaped it, and what could change its behavior now.
Worked numbers three or more rows deep go in the worked table; fewer stay in
prose.
