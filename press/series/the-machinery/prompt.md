# The Machinery

How AI systems actually work, one piece of machinery per article,
hand-worked, no code. If it computes, learns, or decides somewhere between
the arithmetic and the product, it is on the beat. Every mechanism earns its
keep with an example small enough to check on paper. Build on machinery
already explained, link it, and teach only the new part.

Architecture, objective, data, decoding, and the product around them are
different things, and the piece keeps them apart. Say what a component
computes, what shaped it, and what could change it. Worked numbers three or
more rows deep go in the worked table; fewer stay in prose.
