# This paper's shared furniture

This paper has no shared furniture of its own anymore. Its bookends were
promoted to the engine as `nb-bookend` (documented in the lesson template's
`furniture.md`), and its legacy pieces (`rs-prior`, `rs-worked`, `rs-token`)
are retired. Their styles stay in `styles.css` so the published back-catalog
keeps rendering, and the deprecation guard blocks any new article that reaches
for them.
